Hi, I’m Ante Thompson, a Sales & Lease Consultant at Vancouver Honda. My goal is simple: to make buying a vehicle enjoyable, transparent, and completely pressure-free. Before entering the automotive industry, I spent years working in the trades, where honesty, reliability, and attention to detail were essential. Those same values continue to guide the way I work with every customer today. Whether you’re buying your first vehicle, upgrading to something newer, or simply exploring your options, my priority is helping you make the decision that best fits your lifestyle, needs, and budget. I believe the best customer experience comes from listening first, providing straightforward advice, and treating people the way I’d want to be treated. For me, selling a vehicle isn’t about making a sale; it’s about earning your trust and becoming someone you can confidently turn to whenever you need automotive advice in the future. I look forward to helping make your next vehicle purchase a great experience.

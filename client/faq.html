FAQ

Q: Do I need to book an appointment, or can I just walk in?

A: Walk-ins are always welcome, but booking ahead means I can have the right vehicles pulled up and ready for you when you arrive, saving you time on your visit.

Q: Is the free appraisal really free, even if I don't end up buying or trading in?

A: Yes, completely free with no obligation. I'll give you an honest, accurate valuation of your vehicle whether or not you decide to move forward with anything afterward.

Q: I'm not sure exactly what I'm looking for yet, can you still help?

A: That's actually the most common starting point for most of my clients. We'll talk through your budget, daily needs, and lifestyle, and narrow it down together from there.

Q: Do you only sell new vehicles, or can you help me find something used as well?

A: I work with both new and pre-owned inventory, so whether you're looking for the latest model or a reliable used option within a specific budget, I can help you find the right fit.
